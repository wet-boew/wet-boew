Description of Graphic - Organizational Chart - Optimal Content Examples

This is an organizational chart that illustrates how the staff of a company may be organized.

  • The president of the company is Jeanne Hébert.
  • The Vice President of Marketing, Joe Smith, reports to the president and oversees Salesperson 1 and 2.
  • The Vice President of Finance & Admin, Denis Martin, also reports to the president and oversees an administrative assistant and a bookkeeper.
  • The Vice President of Manufacturing reports to the President and oversees a plant foreman.
    • The plant foreman supervises plant workers 1, 2, and 3.
